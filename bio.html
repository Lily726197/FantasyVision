My Biography

I was born at the ProMedica Toledo Hospital on June 19, 2006, along with my twin brother, Luke. We were both diagnosed with autism two years later, which is when we both started to go to preschool at CHiP. We started elementary school at Hill View Elementary in Sylvania in 2012, attending regular classes along with occupational and speech therapy on certain days during the week. We moved to Arbor Hills Junior High in 2018 and Northview High School in 2021, where we are currently in our senior year.
